Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Qariee. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Qariee is designed to work without collecting or storing any personal information about you.

  • We do not require account registration or profile creation
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • We do not track your geographic location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or files

Locally Stored Data

The app stores some data on your device only to improve your experience:

  • Downloaded audio files: Quran surahs you choose to download for offline listening
  • App settings: Your preferences such as last played surah and sleep timer settings
  • Playback state: To resume listening from where you left off

All this data is stored only on your device and is never sent to any external server.

Required Permissions

The app requests the following permissions to perform its core functions:

  • Internet Access: To download and stream audio files from the server
  • Background Playback: To continue playing recitations even when the screen is off
  • Notifications: To display playback controls in the notification bar

Advertising and Tracking

The app contains no advertisements. We also do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools. Our goal is to provide a pure experience for listening to the Holy Quran.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we simply do not collect any data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data Security

All downloaded files and settings are stored securely on your device in the app's private storage area. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its data from your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Qariee is suitable for all ages. We do not collect personal information from any user, including children. The app is safe for use by all family members.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top.
